Impact 25 — Pad a Girl Child

Impact 25 is LLF's flagship outreach in Dogarawa, Zaria (9 Aug 2025). Our goal is to reach 200 girls with education and dignity kits while building systems for sustained access.

Event goals

  • Reach 200 girls with education and kits
  • Engage mothers and leaders to reduce stigma
  • Collect baseline & endline data for evaluation

Planned activities

  1. Registration and welcome
  2. Workshops split by age group
  3. Distribution and hygiene demo
  4. Feedback, referral and close

Kit composition (examples)

Reusable kit
  • 4 cloth pads (handmade locally)
  • Storage bag
  • Soap
  • Information leaflet

Reusable kits are encouraged where feasible to lower long-term costs and build local production.

Disposable kit
  • 8 disposable pads
  • Soap
  • Storage/packaging

Disposable kits suit contexts where access to clean water and safe drying surfaces is limited.

Data & Monitoring

We track attendance, pre/post knowledge, kit satisfaction and referrals. Data is aggregated and shared with partners.

Agenda (detailed)

08:00 — Registration

Sign-in and welcome briefing.

09:00 — Opening remarks

Local leaders and partners address participants.

09:30 — Workshops start

Separate sessions for younger and older adolescents.

12:00 — Distribution

Organized kit distribution and demonstrations.

13:30 — Feedback & close

Group feedback, next steps and linkage to local pharmacies.

Monitoring & Evaluation

Core indicators: attendance numbers, correct answers in knowledge check (pre/post), satisfaction with kits, and follow-up school attendance where available.

Data collection tools

  • Simple attendance registers (paper & digital)
  • Short pre/post quiz forms
  • Volunteer feedback forms

Supply & Partnerships

LLF works with local pharmacies, small-scale manufacturers and women's groups to source kit components. This supports local livelihoods while ensuring local availability for restocking.
