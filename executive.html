Executive Leadership

Profiles of LLF's executive team and advisory board. LLF believes in local leadership, gender balance and technical competence.

Ahmed Abdulsalam

Founder & Executive Director

Ahmed founded LLF after working with community health programs and seeing the recurring barriers girls face during menstruation. Ahmed leads strategy, partnerships and program design for LLF.

Experience: Community health program management, partnership development, stakeholder engagement.

Focus: Strategic direction, partner engagement, fundraising.

Leadership philosophy

LLF chooses leaders based on lived experience, technical skills and commitment to community-driven solutions. We prioritise local recruitment and capacity building to ensure sustainability and community trust.

How to nominate or join

To nominate a leader, provide a short bio, contact details and references. Send submissions to info@lazywingsfoundation.org with subject "Nomination".
